Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Salt 1_spoon
  • Chilli_powder
  • Curd
  • Chicken Masala
  • Oil
  • Turmaric Powder
  • Onions

Instructions

  1. Take bowl
  2. In bowl add chicken,curd,chillipowder,turmericpowder,Masala and oil
  3. Mix all the added ingredients well
  4. After mixing Marinate it upto like 30 minutes
  5. After completing Marination process Take another bowl
  6. And pour some oil in the bowl
  7. And add cutted onions into the bowl.Mix the onions in the oil until onions converts into browniesh
  8. Add the marinated chicken into it mix it well
  9. after some time you can add chilli powder if you want spicy
  10. After some time the tasty chicken is Ready
